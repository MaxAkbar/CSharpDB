Database-generated rowversionconcurrency.rowversion Partial Tier 1

One nonnullable byte[] property per table configured with Timestamp or IsRowVersion is stored as BLOB ROWVERSION. The engine initializes an opaque eight-byte big-endian per-row revision at 1 and advances it on every successful UPDATE, including raw SQL, trigger-issued, and no-op updates. EF refreshes generated tokens and detects stale updates and deletes. Tokens are per-row rather than SQL Server's database-wide counter. Keys, foreign keys, indexes, converters, defaults, computed SQL, multiple rowversion columns, and standalone add or alter rowversion migrations are explicitly rejected.
